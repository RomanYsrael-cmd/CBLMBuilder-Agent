Course Title:
Enterprise Systems

Course Code:
IPE 9

Learning Outcome 1 (LO1):
Explain the purpose, components, and benefits of enterprise systems in organizations.

Topics under LO1:
1. Introduction to Enterprise Systems
   - Definition and Evolution of Enterprise Systems
   - Importance in Modern Organizations
   - Enterprise Systems vs Standalone Systems

2. Components of Enterprise Systems
   - Hardware, Software, Data, and Processes
   - Integration Architecture
   - Centralized Databases

3. Benefits and Roles of Enterprise Systems
   - Organizational Efficiency
   - Decision-Making Support
   - Process Standardization


Learning Outcome 2 (LO2):
Identify different types of enterprise systems and their roles in business process integration.

Topics under LO2:
1. Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)
   - ERP Modules and Functions
   - Integration Across Departments
   - Real-Time Information Systems

2. Supply Chain Management (SCM)
   - Supply Chain Processes
   - Inventory and Logistics Management
   - Supplier Coordination

3. Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
   - Customer Data Management
   - Sales and Marketing Support
   - Customer Service Systems


Learning Outcome 3 (LO3):
Apply enterprise system concepts to analyze and integrate organizational processes.

Topics under LO3:
1. Business Process Integration
   - Cross-functional Workflows
   - Process Standardization
   - End-to-End Business Processes

2. Information Flow and Data Integration
   - Data Sharing Across Departments
   - Real-Time Reporting
   - Data Accuracy and Consistency

3. Designing Enterprise Solutions
   - Aligning Systems with Business Strategy
   - Process Modeling Techniques
   - System Requirements Analysis


Learning Outcome 4 (LO4):
Analyze enterprise system implementation, evaluate performance, and recommend improvements.

Topics under LO4:
1. Implementation Strategies
   - System Selection and Planning
   - Implementation Phases
   - Change Management

2. Challenges and Risks
   - Organizational Resistance
   - Cost and Complexity
   - Technical Risks

3. Evaluation and Continuous Improvement
   - Performance Metrics
   - Scalability and System Effectiveness
   - Continuous Improvement Approaches
