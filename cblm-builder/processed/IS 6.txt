Course Code:
IS 6

Learning Outcome 1 (LO1):
Explain the principles, roles, and processes involved in contact center and IT service management operations.

Topics under LO1:
1. Contact Center Operations
   - Definition and Functions
   - Types of Services
   - Roles of Service Agents

2. Customer Communication Principles
   - Effective Communication Techniques
   - Active Listening and Empathy
   - Professional Standards

3. Service Management Foundations
   - IT Service Management Concepts
   - Role of IS in Services
   - Operational Efficiency


Learning Outcome 2 (LO2):
Demonstrate guided customer service interactions using appropriate language, communication techniques, and service protocols.

Topics under LO2:
1. Customer Service Protocols
   - Greeting Customers
   - Identifying Concerns
   - Call Closing Procedures

2. Service Communication Skills
   - Basic English Communication
   - Tone and Clarity
   - Handling Inquiries

3. Handling Customer Requests
   - Order Taking Procedures
   - Responding to Inquiries
   - Providing Accurate Information


Learning Outcome 3 (LO3):
Apply customer service procedures to resolve inquiries, complaints, and service requests in contact center environments.

Topics under LO3:
1. Managing Customer Inquiries
   - Identifying Needs
   - Providing Solutions
   - Escalation Procedures

2. Handling Customer Complaints
   - Complaint Handling Process
   - Conflict Resolution Techniques
   - Maintaining Professionalism

3. Outbound Telemarketing
   - Telemarketing Principles
   - Customer Follow-ups
   - Ethical Sales Communication


Learning Outcome 4 (LO4):
Evaluate customer service interactions and apply improvements to ensure service quality and customer satisfaction.

Topics under LO4:
1. Service Quality Standards
   - Customer Satisfaction Indicators
   - Performance Metrics
   - Continuous Improvement

2. Professionalism in Customer Service
   - Work Ethics
   - Time Management
   - Team Collaboration

3. Improving Customer Experience
   - Feedback Analysis
   - Service Recovery Strategies
   - CRM Best Practices
