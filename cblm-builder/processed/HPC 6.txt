Course Title:
Foreign Language 2

Course Code:
HPC 6

Learning Outcome 1 (LO1):
Use appropriate expressions in French in daily conversations and workplace settings.

Topics under LO1:
1. Basic Communication
   - Greetings and Introductions
   - Common Courtesy Expressions
   - Basic Conversational Phrases

2. Workplace Vocabulary
   - Jobs and Professions
   - Office Vocabulary
   - Workplace Roles

3. Describing People and Objects
   - Adjectives Usage
   - Gender and Agreement
   - Physical Descriptions


Learning Outcome 2 (LO2):
Read and understand simple texts and dialogues in French.

Topics under LO2:
1. Daily Routine and Time
   - Reflexive Verbs
   - Telling Time
   - Days and Months

2. Workplace Communication
   - Formal vs Informal Speech
   - Workplace Etiquette
   - Basic Dialogues

3. Talking About Schedules
   - Days and Dates
   - Frequency Adverbs
   - Scheduling Expressions


Learning Outcome 3 (LO3):
Write short messages, emails, or notes using correct French grammar.

Topics under LO3:
1. Telephone Conversations
   - Making and Receiving Calls
   - Telephone Expressions
   - Message Taking

2. Giving Directions and Instructions
   - Imperatives
   - Prepositions
   - Instruction Writing

3. Making Appointments
   - Dates and Invitations
   - Polite Requests
   - Appointment Dialogues


Learning Outcome 4 (LO4):
Demonstrate cultural awareness and sensitivity in using the French language.

Topics under LO4:
1. Writing Communication
   - Formal Writing
   - Informal Writing
   - Common Expressions

2. Talking About Past Events
   - Passé Composé
   - Auxiliary Verbs
   - Sentence Construction

3. Customer Service Situations
   - Assisting Customers
   - Handling Complaints
   - Offering Solutions
