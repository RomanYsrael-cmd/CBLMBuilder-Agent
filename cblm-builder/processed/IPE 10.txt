Course Title:
Supply Chain Management

Course Code:
IPE 10

Learning Outcome 1 (LO1):
Comprehend the strategic role of SCM and the impact of integrated information systems on organizational flow.

Topics under LO1:
1. Evolution and Strategy of SCM
   - Internal vs. External Supply Chains
   - The Bullwhip Effect
   - Competitive Advantage through SCM

2. Digital Supply Chain Infrastructure
   - Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)
   - Electronic Data Interchange (EDI)
   - IoT and Blockchain in Traceability

3. Foundations of Supply Chain Management
   - Strategic Role of SCM
   - Organizational Flow Integration
   - Information Systems Support


Learning Outcome 2 (LO2):
Execute operational planning for procurement and inventory management using quantitative and qualitative optimization techniques.

Topics under LO2:
1. Strategic Sourcing and Procurement
   - Supplier Selection and Evaluation
   - E-procurement Systems
   - Contract Management and Ethics

2. Inventory Management Models
   - Economic Order Quantity (EOQ)
   - Just-in-Time vs. Just-in-Case
   - Safety Stock Calculation

3. Material Requirements Planning (MRP)
   - Bill of Materials (BOM)
   - Master Production Schedule (MPS)
   - Capacity Requirement Planning


Learning Outcome 3 (LO3):
Develop integrated logistics and distribution workflows that maximize customer value while minimizing carbon footprint.

Topics under LO3:
1. Transportation and Logistics Management
   - Intermodal Transportation
   - Last-Mile Delivery Challenges
   - Third-Party Logistics (3PL) and 4PL

2. Warehousing and Distribution Center Operations
   - Warehouse Layout Design
   - Cross-Docking Strategies
   - Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems

3. Green Supply Chain and Circular Economy
   - Reverse Logistics
   - Reducing Carbon Footprint in Shipping
   - Sustainable Packaging Solutions


Learning Outcome 4 (LO4):
Critique supply chain resilience and performance using data-driven analytics to mitigate risks and propose sustainable improvements.

Topics under LO4:
1. Supply Chain Risk Management
   - Identifying Global Disruptions
   - Contingency Planning
   - Resiliency vs. Robustness

2. SCM Performance Measurement
   - SCOR Model
   - Key Performance Indicators in Logistics
   - Benchmarking

3. Continuous Improvement and Future Trends
   - Lean and Six Sigma in SCM
   - Artificial Intelligence and Predictive Analytics
   - Globalization vs. Regionalization
