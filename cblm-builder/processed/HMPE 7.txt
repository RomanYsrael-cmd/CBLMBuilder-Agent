Course Title:
Classical French Cuisine

Course Code:
HMPE 7

Learning Outcome 1 (LO1):
Understand the history and cultural influence of Classical French Cuisine.

Topics under LO1:
1. Introduction to Classical French Cuisine
   - History
   - Escoffier’s Brigade System
   - Cuisine vs Culture

2. French Kitchen Hierarchy & Terminology
   - Chef roles
   - Common French culinary terms

3. French Culinary Techniques I
   - Knife cuts
   - Mise en place
   - Sautéing
   - Poaching


Learning Outcome 2 (LO2):
Demonstrate mastery of foundational French cooking techniques (e.g., sauces, stocks, knife skills).

Topics under LO2:
1. Stocks and Sauces
   - White stocks
   - Brown stocks
   - Vegetable stocks
   - Mother sauces


Learning Outcome 3 (LO3):
Prepare classical French dishes with proper presentation and adherence to tradition.

Topics under LO3:
1. Soups
   - Classic French soups
   - Consommé
   - Bisque
   - Potage

2. French Herbs and Spices
   - Common herbs
   - Flavor pairings

3. Appetizers & Hors d’oeuvres
   - Terrines
   - Pâtés
   - Quiches
   - Canapés

4. Poultry Dishes
   - Coq au Vin
   - Duck à l’Orange

5. Beef and Lamb Dishes
   - Slow-cooking methods
   - Braising methods
   - Boeuf Bourguignon
   - Navarin d’Agneau

6. Seafood in French Cuisine
   - Traditional seafood techniques
   - Bouillabaisse
   - Sole Meunière

7. Vegetables and Side Dishes
   - Classical vegetable dishes
   - Quiche Lorraine
   - Soufflé

8. French Breads
   - Fermentation
   - Bread techniques
   - Baguette
   - Brioche

9. French Desserts
   - Classic French desserts
   - Crème brûlée
   - Tarte Tatin
   - Madeleines


Learning Outcome 4 (LO4):
Appreciate and respect French culinary traditions and regional diversity.

Topics under LO4:
1. Regional French Cuisine
   - Compare regional ingredients and styles
   - Provence
   - Normandy
   - Alsace
   - Burgundy
