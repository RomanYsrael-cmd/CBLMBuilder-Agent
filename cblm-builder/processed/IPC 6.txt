Course Title:
Evaluation of Business Performance

Course Code:
IPC 6

Learning Outcome 1 (LO1):
Categorize various performance measurement frameworks and the technological infrastructure required to support data-driven management.

Topics under LO1:
1. Foundations of KPIs and Metrics
   - Leading vs. Lagging Indicators
   - SMART Criteria for Metrics
   - Qualitative vs. Quantitative Performance Data

2. Performance Management Systems Architecture
   - Balanced Scorecard Approach
   - Objectives and Key Results (OKRs)
   - Data Flow in IS-enabled Performance Monitoring

3. Foundations of Business Performance Evaluation
   - Role of Information Systems in Performance Management
   - Data-Driven Management Concepts
   - Organizational Performance Frameworks


Learning Outcome 2 (LO2):
Execute quantitative analysis using financial ratios and statistical methods to diagnose organizational health and operational trends.

Topics under LO2:
1. Financial Ratio Analysis
   - Liquidity, Solvency, and Profitability Ratios
   - Horizontal and Vertical Trend Analysis
   - Benchmarking against Industry Standards

2. Operational Efficiency Metrics
   - Inventory Turnover and Supply Chain Velocity
   - Employee Productivity Ratios
   - Customer Acquisition Cost vs. Lifetime Value

3. Predictive Analytics for Business Trends
   - Regression Analysis for Sales Forecasting
   - Variance Analysis (Actual vs. Budget)
   - Seasonal Trend Identification


Learning Outcome 3 (LO3):
Utilize Business Intelligence (BI) software to create interactive visualizations and dashboards for real-time performance monitoring.

Topics under LO3:
1. Data Integration and ETL Processes
   - Extract, Transform, and Load (ETL) Basics
   - Cleaning Dirty Data for Analysis
   - Connecting Multiple Data Silos

2. Dashboard Design and Storytelling
   - Principles of Visual Perception
   - Choosing the Right Chart Type
   - Designing for Executive vs. Operational Viewers

3. Real-time Monitoring and Alerting
   - Setting Thresholds and Red-Flag Alerts
   - Automated Reporting Workflows
   - Mobile BI Accessibility


Learning Outcome 4 (LO4):
Architect a comprehensive performance management system that aligns departmental metrics with high-level strategic goals and sustainability standards.

Topics under LO4:
1. Strategic Alignment and Cascading Metrics
   - Linking Departmental KPIs to Corporate Strategy
   - Managing Conflicting Metrics
   - Incentive Systems and Performance

2. Governance, Ethics, and Data Privacy
   - Ethics in Performance Tracking
   - Data Security in BI Systems
   - Compliance with Data Protection Laws

3. System Implementation and Change Management
   - Roll-out Strategies for New Evaluation Systems
   - Training Stakeholders
   - Continuous Improvement of the PMS
