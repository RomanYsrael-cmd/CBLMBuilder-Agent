Course Title:
Organization and Management Concepts

Course Code:
IPC 4

Learning Outcome 1 (LO1):
Explain the fundamental principles of management and the structure of organizations in different business environments.

Topics under LO1:
1. Introduction to Organizations
   - Definition and Characteristics of Organizations
   - Types of Organizations
   - Organizational Goals and Objectives

2. Principles of Management
   - Evolution of Management Thought
   - Classical Management Principles
   - Importance of Management

3. Foundations of Organizations
   - Organizational Environment
   - Role of Managers
   - Organizational Effectiveness


Learning Outcome 2 (LO2):
Identify and explain the major functions of management in organizational operations.

Topics under LO2:
1. Planning
   - Strategic Planning
   - Tactical and Operational Planning
   - Goal Setting and Forecasting

2. Organizing
   - Resource Allocation
   - Departmentalization
   - Coordination and Authority

3. Leading and Controlling
   - Leadership and Motivation
   - Communication in Organizations
   - Performance Monitoring and Control


Learning Outcome 3 (LO3):
Interpret organizational charts, management hierarchies, and departmental roles in business organizations.

Topics under LO3:
1. Organizational Charts and Hierarchies
   - Types of Organizational Charts
   - Chain of Command
   - Span of Control

2. Business Departments
   - Human Resources Management
   - Marketing and Sales
   - Finance and Operations

3. Management Approaches
   - Bureaucratic Management
   - Contemporary Management Practices
   - Agile and Adaptive Management


Learning Outcome 4 (LO4):
Analyze managerial decision-making processes and design simple organizational structures for business scenarios.

Topics under LO4:
1. Decision-Making in Organizations
   - Types of Managerial Decisions
   - Decision-Making Models
   - Problem-Solving Techniques

2. Leadership and Conflict Management
   - Leadership Styles
   - Team Dynamics
   - Conflict Resolution

3. Designing Organizational Structures
   - Factors Influencing Design
   - Aligning Structure with Strategy
   - Evaluating Effectiveness
