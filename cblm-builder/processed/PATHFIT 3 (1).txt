Course Title:
Sports Fitness

Course Code:
PATHFIT 3

Learning Outcome 1 (LO1):
Have a clear understanding on the basic information regarding volleyball.

Topics under LO1:
1. Orientation and Course Overview
   - Nature of the subject
   - Expectations
   - Grading system and requirements
   - Course introduction activities
   - Reflection and expectation sharing

2. Basic Information on Volleyball
   - Brief history and development
   - Benefits of playing volleyball
   - Facilities and equipment
   - Terminologies

3. Learning Activities and Assessments
   - Written tests
   - Practical tests
   - Group activities
   - Reflection papers


Learning Outcome 2 (LO2):
Review and refine basic skills used in playing volleyball.

Topics under LO2:
1. Basic Volleyball Skills
   - Ball handling
   - Passing and receiving
   - Serving
   - Spiking
   - Blocking

2. Rules and Officiating
   - Basic rules and regulations
   - Officials’ duties and responsibilities
   - Officiating hand signals

3. Skill Application and Games
   - Lead-up games
   - Individual, dual, and group performance
   - Practical drills and modified games


Learning Outcome 3 (LO3):
Perform the game properly with mastery and enthusiasm.

Topics under LO3:
1. Tournament Systems
   - Single elimination
   - Double elimination
   - Round robin

2. Game Scheduling and Organization
   - Organizing matches
   - Tracking results
   - Comparing tournament formats

3. Game Execution
   - Applying rules in gameplay
   - Mastery and performance
   - Strategic play


Learning Outcome 4 (LO4):
Strengthen one's personality through interaction with different members of the team.

Topics under LO4:
1. Actual Gameplay
   - Tournament proper
   - Elimination games
   - Semi-final games
   - Championship

2. Teamwork and Leadership
   - Leadership and followership
   - Cooperation
   - Sportsmanship

3. Personal Development
   - Accepting results
   - Confidence building
   - Social interaction
