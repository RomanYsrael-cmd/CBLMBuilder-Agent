Course Title: Catering Management
Course Code: CATE3
Learning Outcome 1: Plan and Control Catering Operations for Diverse
Food Services

Topics: 
A. Introduction to Catering Operations
Subtopics: 
- Scope and importance of catering 
- Roles in catering management 
- Overview of TESDA Food Production NC III

B. Menu Planning: Principles & Trends 
Subtopics: 
- Types of menus (a la
carte, cycle, buffet) 
- Thematic menu design 
- Culinary trends

C. Menu Costing and Pricing 
Subtopics: 
- Costing recipes 
- Food cost percentage 
- Pricing strategies

D. Catering Controls & Inventory Management 
Subtopics: 
- Portion control 
- Inventory systems (FIFO, stock cards) 
- Waste reduction

Learning Outcome 2: Supervise and Implement Food Quality and Sanitation Standards

Topics: 
A. Food Quality Control 
Subtopics: 
- Establishing standards 
- Sensory evaluation 
- Inspection and corrective action

B. Sanitation and Cleaning Procedures 
Subtopics: 
- Cleaning schedules 
- HACCP principles 
- Workplace safety and hygiene

C. Menu Consolidation and Execution of Planned Menu 
Subtopics: 
- Consolidate menu + control + sanitation 
- Assign roles and production tasks 
- Execution of planned menu 
- Supervision of cleaning routines 
- Application of costing and controls

Learning Outcome 3: Design and Execute Thematic and Specialty Cuisine Menus

Topics: 
A. Specialty Cuisines 
Subtopics: 
- International cuisine overview 
- Traditional vs modern plating 
- Cultural food handling

B. Garde Manger & Cold Kitchen 
Subtopics: 
- Cold appetizers 
- Salads and dressings 
- Pâtés and terrines

C. Bakery & Patisserie 
Subtopics: 
- Tarts, choux pastry, entremets 
- Dessert styling 
- Storage for delicate items

Learning Outcome 4: Manage Large-Scale Food Production and Beverage Service for Events

Topics: 
A. Beverage & Wine Knowledge 
Subtopics: 
- Beverage classifications 
- Food pairing techniques 
- Non-alcoholic alternatives

B. Buffet Planning & Layout 
ubtopics: 
- Service styles (French,
Russian, buffet) 
- Equipment & layout 
- Timing and flow

C. Bulk Cooking Operations 
Subtopics: 
- Assorted textures: crisp, chewy, nutty

D. Final Event Planning 
Subtopics: 
- Consolidate all outputs 
- Finalize theme, roles, dishes 
- Rehearse plating and service
