Course Title:
Business Process Management

Course Code:
IPC 5

Learning Outcome 1 (LO1):
Categorize the foundational theories of BPM and differentiate the stages of the Business Process Lifecycle.

Topics under LO1:
1. Evolution and Principles of BPM
   - History of Process Management
   - Process-Centered Organization
   - BPM vs Project Management

2. Business Process Lifecycle
   - Discovery and Modeling
   - Analysis and Redesign
   - Implementation and Monitoring

3. Foundations of BPM
   - Role of BPM in Organizations
   - Strategic Alignment
   - Continuous Improvement Concepts


Learning Outcome 2 (LO2):
Construct professional-grade process models using BPMN 2.0 to represent "As-Is" and "To-Be" organizational workflows.

Topics under LO2:
1. BPMN Basic Syntax
   - Events (Start, Intermediate, End)
   - Activities and Tasks
   - Gateways (Exclusive, Parallel, Inclusive)

2. Advanced Modeling Objects
   - Pools and Lanes
   - Sub-processes and Call Activities
   - Data Objects and Data Stores

3. As-Is vs To-Be Modeling
   - Current State Documentation
   - Visualization of Improvements
   - Gap Analysis Techniques


Learning Outcome 3 (LO3):
Perform quantitative and qualitative process analysis to identify bottlenecks, waste, and automation opportunities.

Topics under LO3:
1. Qualitative Process Analysis
   - Value-Added Analysis
   - Root Cause Analysis
   - Waste Identification

2. Quantitative Process Analysis
   - Flow Analysis and Cycle Time
   - Little’s Law
   - Process Simulation

3. Process Redesign Heuristics
   - Parallelism and Resequencing
   - Task Elimination
   - System Integration for Efficiency


Learning Outcome 4 (LO4):
Formulate a comprehensive process improvement plan that integrates workflow automation and performance monitoring systems.

Topics under LO4:
1. Process Automation and Workflow Systems
   - BPM Systems (BPMS)
   - Robotic Process Automation (RPA)
   - Service-Oriented Architecture

2. Performance Monitoring and Analytics
   - Process Mining
   - Key Performance Indicators
   - Real-time Dashboards

3. Governance and Continuous Improvement
   - Six Sigma and TQM
   - Process Governance Roles
   - Sustaining Improvements
